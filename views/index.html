Use this service to verify your ID documents and generate a proof of identity certificate.

You have the option to share your identification documents with other services.

How long it takes

It will take around 5-10 minutes to complete this form.

There will be a follow up appointment to verify your ID. This will last no longer than 5 minutes and you can book this at a time that suits you.

Before you start

You will need two examples of ID

  • one photographic ID
  • one proof of address

See examples here.

You will also need access to a smartphone or a digital camera to take a photo of your ID.
